HD Map as a Service

What is HD Map as a Service？

Why choose HDMS？

NavInfo HD Map Service (HDMS) is an online cloud service comprised of tile-based layers with lane-level map data. It provides various information of surrounding environment, including highly accurate description of static environment information, dynamic information and other information provided by OEM or 3rd Party. The data in HDMS is continuously updated to meet the needs of autonomous driving to obtain the freshest environmental information.

How do we keep the HD Map data fresh?

As is well known, the freshness of HD Map data is essential to the safe autonomous driving. It impacts many systems including correct environmental modeling, planning of vehicle control maneuvers, execution of vehicle control. If changes of reality can not be updated promptly, neither the reliability of map nor the safety of the vehicles can be guaranteed. To minimize the time between the change detection and completion of map updates, the perfect cloud service for the close loop of production, crowdsourcing and delivering has been made for the update of HD Map data.
