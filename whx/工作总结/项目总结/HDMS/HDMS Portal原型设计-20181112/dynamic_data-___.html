Dynamic Content

In contrast to static map, dynamic data represents the dynamic situation of the road network. As a necessary complement for more reliable and safer infotainment Navigation and Highly-Automated Driving, dynamic data promotes the reliable of route planning and the safe autonomous driving by providing an accurate description of the real-time changing driving environment.

Dynamic data mainly includes two parts of information, traffic incidents (a traffic congestion, an accident location) and traffic regulation (hazardous driving condition ahead, variable speed limit, tunnel and bridge closures, overtaking restriction, reversible lanes, etc).

In order to ensure the reliability of dynamic information to the greatest extent, we mainly obtain dynamic information from the following sources.


• Event Type: road closure, roadwork, traffic control

• Life Cycle: long period

• Coverage: 60+cities, 2000+ events per month


Official government websites

Traffic Radio

• Life Cycle: short period

• Coverage: 60+cities, 200000+ events per month

• Life Cycle: long period

• Coverage: 340+cities, 20000~30000 events per month

Local Project

• Life Cycle: short period and long period

• Coverage: Shanghai Information Center, Suzhou Traffic Police, Xi'an Traffic Police, 5000+ events per month
