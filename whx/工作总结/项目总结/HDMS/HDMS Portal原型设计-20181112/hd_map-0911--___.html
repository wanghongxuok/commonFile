HD Map

Overview

Application scenarios

NavInfo HD Map is a full featured map product for autonomous driving. HD Map is rich in high-precision features and attributes, covering highway and urban road. At present, more than 100 features/attributes are represented in HD Map. Various formats are available, such as MIF, NDS. HD Map can satisfy the requirements of autonomous driving in multi-scenarios according to customers’ feedback.

Obstacle detection and avoidance

To provide precise guidance instructions and advanced lane guidance functionality, navigation systems for highly automated driving need detailed information on road topology and road geometry.

High accurate localization (lateral, longitudinal and Z-Level) on lane level within the map using content from localisation layer

High accurate planning with all information of the real world represented within the map

High accurate road topology and geometry on lane level for trajectory planning
